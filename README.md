# Capstone 3 — EmporiUm South Region Sales Analysis

A Power BI report analyzing sales for EmporiUm's South region (Florida, South Carolina, and Texas) from 2022 to 2025, covering both in-store and online sales. Built for the regional management team to highlight sales trends, top categories, and best-selling books.

**Analyst:** Hamzah Alssoudi
**Program:** Year Up United Data Analytics Training Academy — Week 12 Capstone

## Project Overview
This report pulls together four years of in-store and online sales for the South region, cleans and combines the data, and presents the key findings through four interactive visuals across two report pages.

## Data Sources
- `Capstone3_Sample_Sales.xlsx` — Store Sales, Online Sales, Products, Store Locations
- `book_list.txt` — general-audience book titles and authors

## Report Contents
- Line chart — total sales trend over time (2022–2025)
- Column chart — sales by product category
- Donut chart — sales by state (Florida, South Carolina, Texas)
- Table — top-selling general books with author names
- Year slicer — interactive filter applied across the report

## Key Insights
- Technology & Accessories drives about 73% of total sales.
- Sales nearly doubled in 2025 compared to prior years.
- Texas is the strongest state, followed by Florida.
- Online and in-store sales are nearly even, giving the region a balanced mix.
